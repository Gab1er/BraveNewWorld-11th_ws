Security ?

Once in the reservation, how can I be sure that savages will not try to hurt me?

Dear visitor, once in the park you should never worry about security. You are primarily here to advance your researches and certainly not to be hurt..

This is why, the World State made sure, before opening Malpais New Mexico Reservation, that savages will never, in any case, harm a visitor. However, if you feel in danger at any moment be sure to go to the nearest rest-house to contact our Reservation Guards.
